Nim Game | AI | Alpha-beta Algorithm

Engage with our AI-driven implementation of the Nim game, utilizing the alpha-beta pruning algorithm to enhance gameplay intelligence. This classic strategy game, adapted for artificial intelligence, challenges players to outsmart a highly capable AI opponent. The alpha-beta pruning algorithm is integral to our AI's design, optimizing decision-making processes by efficiently evaluating millions of possible moves and countermoves. This not only speeds up the AI’s response time but also significantly deepens the strategic complexity of the game.
